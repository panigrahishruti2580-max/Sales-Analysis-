# 📊 Sales Analysis Project

## Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales data to uncover business insights, identify growth opportunities, and support data-driven decision-making. The analysis includes revenue trends, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and regional sales performance.

---

## Business Problem Statement

Organizations generate large volumes of sales data across products, customers, regions, and sales channels. However, without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to identify key revenue drivers, profitable customer segments, top-performing products, and areas requiring improvement.

This project aims to transform raw sales data into actionable insights that help businesses improve sales performance, optimize profitability, and support strategic decision-making.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance.
* Identify top-performing products and customers.
* Evaluate regional and channel-wise sales.
* Measure profitability and profit margins.
* Discover trends and seasonal patterns.
* Provide business recommendations based on data insights.

---
